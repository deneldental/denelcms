PATIENT/GUARDIAN ACKNOWLEDGMENT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I, _________________________________, hereby consent to orthodontic treatment
for myself/my child as recommended and explained by the treating orthodontist.

I understand and acknowledge the following:

1. TREATMENT DURATION
   • The treatment duration may vary depending on individual response
   • Cooperation and compliance are essential for timely completion
   • Missing appointments may extend treatment time

2. ORAL HYGIENE
   • Proper oral hygiene is essential during orthodontic treatment
   • Poor hygiene can lead to cavities, gum disease, and tooth discoloration
   • I will maintain regular dental check-ups and cleanings

3. APPOINTMENTS
   • Regular appointments are necessary for successful treatment
   • I will notify the clinic at least 24 hours in advance if I need to reschedule
   • Frequent missed appointments may result in treatment delays

4. CARE INSTRUCTIONS
   • I will follow all care instructions provided by the orthodontist
   • I will avoid hard, sticky, and sugary foods as instructed
   • I will wear retainers as prescribed after active treatment

5. POTENTIAL RISKS AND DISCOMFORT
   • Mild discomfort and soreness may occur, especially after adjustments
   • Temporary speech changes may occur with certain appliances
   • Rare complications may include root resorption, tooth mobility, or TMJ issues

6. RETAINER PHASE
   • Retainer wear is essential to maintain results
   • Failure to wear retainers may result in teeth shifting back
   • Retainer replacement costs are my responsibility

7. FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY
   • I understand the treatment costs and payment plan
   • I am responsible for any missed appointment fees
   • I will cover costs for lost or damaged appliances
